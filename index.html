Data analyst || Risk analyst

Department of Customs, Government of Nepal

I am a data analyst with over 10 years of experience working in the government sector. I hold a Master’s degree in Mathematics. My expertise lies in using SQL, R, Python, and Power BI to collect, clean, and analyze large sets of data, identify patterns and trends, and develop data-driven strategies to support organizational goals. I have been recognized for my work by being awarded as the best employee and best teacher in my career. I am confident that my skills and experience make me an excellent candidate for any datarelated role. Please feel free to contact me to discuss how I can help your organization achieve its goals through data-driven insights.
