Biography

Mohan Pudasaini is a WCO-accredited expert in two areas, namely: Customs Risk Management and Data Analytics, with over a decade of public sector experience. Currently at Nepal Customs as Risk/Data Analyst, he leverages SQL, R, Python, and Tableau to transform complex data into actionable insights, streamlining risk assessment and compliance processes. As a trainer, he has led 40+ capacity-building workshops.

In his professional journey, he has earned Best Teacher and Best Employee awards. Actively engaged in WCO initiatives, he contributes to international best practices in customs administration. Passionate about data-driven governance, he remains committed to advancing analytics and risk management for smarter customs operations globally.
