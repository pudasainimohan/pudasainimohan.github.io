Biography

I am a data analyst based in Kathmandu, Nepal. With over a decade of experience in the public sector, I have honed my skills in utilizing SQL, R, Python, and Power BI to manage, process, and analyze large datasets. My expertise and experience make me a confident and highly capable candidate for any role in the data analysis and data science field. Outside of my professional life, I am passionate about using data to drive decision-making and drive positive change. I am dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest developments in the field, and am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and knowledge.
