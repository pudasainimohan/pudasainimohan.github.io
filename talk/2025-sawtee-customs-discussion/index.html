Knowledge Sharing Session with SAWTEE


Date
Aug 14, 2025 3:00 PM — 5:00 PM
Event
Knowledge Sharing Session with SAWTEE
Location
Kathmandu, Nepal

I had the privilege of delivering a knowledge sharing and discussion session at SAWTEE (South Asia Watch on Trade, Economics & Environment), a leading trade, economics, and policy research organization in South Asia.

It was an honour to have Dr. Posh Raj Pandey, Chair Emeritus, Mr. Paras Kharel, PhD, Executive Director, and the research officers of SAWTEE present during the session. I drew upon my expertise, including my work as a WCO-accredited expert in Risk Management and Data Analytics, to share insights and foster meaningful exchanges.

The interactive session covered:

  • Role of Customs in Global Trade
  • How Customs is driven by technology worldwide, with practical use cases
  • Opportunities and challenges in using AI for Customs operations, including insights from my recent research on Using AI to Identify and Expedite Environmental Goods at Customs
  • Recent trends in cross-border trade

My sincere thanks to SAWTEE for organizing this engaging event and inviting me as the speaker.
