Advancing Customs and Tax Practices with Data Analytics and AI


Date
Dec 26, 2024 10:00 AM — 12:00 PM
Event
In-service training for government official.
Location
Kathmandu, Nepal
Kathmandu,

On December 26, 2024, I had the privilege of leading a session on “Data Analytics and AI in Customs” at the Public Finance Management Training Center, Nepal. This event brought together more than 35 officials from various Customs and Tax offices, creating an enriching environment for knowledge-sharing and collaboration.

During the session, we delved into global trends, key World Customs Organization (WCO) initiatives, and the practical applications of data analytics and artificial intelligence within the customs. Key focus areas included risk management, fraud detection, and trade facilitation. The session also highlighted how Nepal Customs is integrating these advanced technologies into its operations to improve efficiency and compliance.

What made this experience particularly fulfilling was the active engagement and insightful contributions from the participants. This exchange of ideas reaffirmed the pivotal role of data-driven approaches in shaping the future of customs and tax administration.

I’m grateful for the opportunity to contribute to this important training initiative.
